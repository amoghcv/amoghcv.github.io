  • overview

  • We are looking for a Game Designer with passion for video games to join us and to work on our games. As a Game Designer, you will have the chance to work on some cool titles and have the opportunity to craft interesting experiences. You get to understand player behaviour and craft experiences with will keep players hooked to the game. In collaboration with both internal and external partners, you will be a core contributor working on game features from beginning to end, until the feature makes it into the game. In a small and close-knit team, at times, many hats may need to be worn and the ideal candidate should feel comfortable with that. The candidate will bring expertise and will be supported by highly skilled colleagues deeply rooted in the games industry.

  • responsibilities
    • Work closely with other designers and cross-functionally with UX, engineering, and product management on the design and implementation of new game features.
    • Build and lead the implementation of fun, engaging in-game events and other game content.
    • Build clear and complete game design documentation for game content and features.
    • Assess and apply qualitative and quantitative feedback to improve game design and balance.
    • Work closely with product managers, producers, engineers and artists to design and prototype new gameplay and systems.
    • Be the advocate of good design practices across the studio.
    • Identify process improvements where needed and work with the team to drive the implementation of the process improvements.

  • candidate profile
    • 3 plus years experience in mobile game design, including at least one year on a game in live ops.
    • Deep understanding of games and what makes them fun, both as gamer and a professional.
    • Experience building in-game content designed to appeal to a specific audience.
    • Strong understanding or interest in online multiplayer games.
    • Deep understanding of basic economy balance and tuning.
    • Experience writing clear and concise specs, user flows, and feature documentation.
    • Solid knowledge of free-to-play games in general and the puzzle genre in greater detail.
    • Proactively identify issues with product or processes and collaborate with team to solve them.
    • Excellent written and verbal communication skills in English.
    • Passion for games and the entertainment industry.
    Plusses:
    • First-hand knowledge of gameplay, progression and transaction mechanics across a variety of genres.
    • Curiosity to understand "why" game mechanics are effective and why players behave the way they do.
    • Internal motivation to explore problems and has an ability to find unique ways to solve them.
