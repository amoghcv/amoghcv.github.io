  • overview

  • We're looking for an experienced Senior Concept Artist who wants to work on our Mobile games. As a Senior Concept Artist you will be an expert in your field. Working as part of a focused team, you will be able to creatively express ideas quickly and effectively and be able to adapt very quickly to established work process, set styles and work deadlines.

  • responsibilities
    • Create outstanding concept artwork that helps guide the tone, mood and aesthetic as defined by the Art Director.
    • Create character, creature, weapon, vehicle and item concepts including detailed callouts for production-ready asset creation.
    • Iterate on game assets via paintovers, studies and group critiques to improve overall art quality of any given element.
    • Take art and design requirements and provide creative solutions to challenging briefs.
    • Help others to consistently reach that bar by mentoring and guiding more junior or less experienced colleagues.

  • candidate profile
    • A minimum of three years' experience in game development as a 2D Artist specialising in game art/illustration.
    • Strong understanding of digital art creation tools such as Photoshop and Painter.
    • An excellent foundation in illustration skills and deep understanding of human/creature anatomy, colour theory, shape and form, language and silhouette.
    • Great eye for light, value, composition, colour, staging, architecture and detail in environmental design.
    • Comfortable working in a predefined art style or able to explore the undefined.
    • Self-motivated with excellent communication skills and a great team player attitude.
    • Able to effectively collaborate with game designers and art teams.
    • Creative and passionate approach to problem-solving.
    • Able to take and interpret direction and feedback.
    • Passion for gaming and making games.
    Plusses:
    • Has shipped several AAA console/PC titles as a Senior Concept Artist.
    • Understanding of 3D pipelines (Zbrush, Blender, Maya experience).
    • A love of stylised artwork/games.
