Senior DevOps engineer

  • overview

  • We're looking for an experienced and hands-on Senior DevOps Engineer who wishes to own the devops pipeline of our games. In a small and close-knit team many hats need to be worn and the ideal candidate should feel comfortable with that. The candidate will bring strong technical expertise and will be supported by highly skilled colleagues deeply rooted in the games industry.

  • responsibilities
    • Take ownership of entire features and components of the game. Collaborate with members of the team to deliver large and complex features in time and at very high quality.
    • Be part of the development process and contribute improvements and suggestions, maintain code quality through the whole production cycle.
    • Mentor other developers in the team and help maintain code, product, and process quality.
    • Provide input to Art, Design and Production to ensure schedules and goals meet business and project needs.
    • Conduct regular code reviews of feature code and be part of the hiring process for future colleagues.
    • Identify process improvements where needed and work with the team to drive the implementation of the process improvements.

    • 5+ years experience working with large-scale, cloud based infrastructures.
    • Experience with cost, performance optimization of both legacy and modern infrastructures.

    • Extensive experience with security-minded cloud networking & Extensive experience working with legacy infrastructures.
    • Extensive experience in Git, Docker and Jenkins pipelines.
    • Extensive experience with self-testing to minimize and fix issues.
    • Experience with "Infrastructure as Code" methodology.
    • Experience with configuration management tools (Chef, Ansible, Salt, etc).
    • Experience with High-Load or Data-Intensive applications.

    • Experienced in mentoring other developers in the team.
