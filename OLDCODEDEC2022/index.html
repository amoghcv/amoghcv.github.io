longbow

Longbow was founded by passionate, creative and experienced game industry veterans with a track record of creating very successful and high quality F2P games on mobile devices.

Longbow brings together, as a team, talented individuals who give us the ability to take the long shot and focus on what matters most, player delight. At Longbow, we value our employees, our work and our players and believe that if we take care of these three things, everything else will fall in place. We firmly believe in focus, precision and looking at the big picture.

We are located in the beautiful, idyllic city of Mysuru, in the Southern Indian state of Karnataka. Mysuru is one of the top three Indian cities when it comes to cleanliness and quality of living and does not suffer the many issues that plague the neighbouring city of Bangalore (water crisis, rising population, high cost of living). Mysuru is a city which has something for everybody. Be it the shopper, the traveller or the businessman. So come, work with us in Mysuru and fall in love with Longbow, this beautiful city and a better quality of life.

Services

Longbow specialises in Game development and providing services related to game development. We have a talented in-house development team which can explore product possibilities and set a solid direction for the product after identifying risks and unknowns and either eliminating them or coming up with a plan to address them. We believe in testing and validating our hypothesis early on and our extensive experience with F2P gives us a wide array of tools and knowledge to be able to do this in an effective manner and identify and address risks.
