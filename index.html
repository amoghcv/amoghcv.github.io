About Us

Longbow is founded by passionate, creative and experienced game industry veterans with a track record of creating very successful and high quality F2P games on mobile.

Longbow brings together, as a team, talented individuals who give us the ability to take the long shot and focus on what matters most, player delight. At Longbow, we value our employees, our work and our players and believe that if we take care of these three things, everything else will fall in place. We firmly believe in focus, precision and looking at the big picture.

Broad Vision of Games

Longbow is focusing on making engaging casual games for mobile that deliver delightful player experiences. We focus on polishing all aspects of our games with the singular purpose of delivering a polished experience; be it the game design, art or any other aspect of the game.

We have a talented team which explores game possibilities and sets a solid direction for the product early in the process. We believe in testing and validating our hypothesis early and our extensive experience with F2P enables us to do this in an effective manner.
