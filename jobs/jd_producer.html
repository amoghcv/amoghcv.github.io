  • overview
  • We're looking for an experienced Game Producer who wants to lead / assist game development production of our Mobile games. As a game producer you will use your understanding and passion for game production to create a roadmap for the game which helps bring the creative vision of the game to life. You will also be responsivle to execute this roadmap in collaboration with the development team. You love and live games and are an expert communicator and an effective project coordinator who has experience managing games in various stages of production.

    You need to drive team culture by initiating, fostering, maintaining, and managing others towards positive working relationships within the team and with partners who are critical to both current and future development process. You must be a great team player and help form a stimulating and fun work environment.
  • responsibilities
    • Own the schedule - Build and maintain project plans, schedules, and roadmaps considering dependencies across multiple development teams to ensure successful execution of schedule.
    • Transparency - Provide transparency on project status and tracking risks for stakeholders in order to ensure they are properly informed of any impacts from upcoming releases.
    • Own the outcome
    • Work closely with your game team and partners to identify improvements in existing processes and/or to craft new processes to efficiently produce high quality work on time and on budget. You will also manage and drive these process changes across your team or across the organization.
    • Passionately articulate the core experience and vision, ensure alignment within your team and help create a fun, engaging and stimulating work environment.
    • Owning the processes and seeking continuous improvement. Running the basics of the project: stand-ups, scheduling meetings, and taking notes.
    • Identify and cost risks appropriately, and communicate all roadblocks and bottlenecks that may prevent developers from achieving established goals in the allotted time.
    • Work with top management to determine resources necessary to see projects through to completion.
    • Communicate frequently with the top management on project status, risks, and opportunities.
    • Support the team with production tools.

  • candidate profile
    • Bachelor’s level degree (BA, BSc, BCom, BBM etc.) in any subject.
    • 3+ years of game production experience on Mobile or Web, or eqivalent years of running operations and solving complex problems for a large scale consumer application product.
    • Exceptional verbal and written communications skills. Ability to communicate effectively with all production disciplines, particularly with programming / technical personnel
    • Demonstrated ability to lead multi-functional teams to efficiently deliver high-quality features and experiences on time and on budget.
    • An enthusiastic, passionate, performance-driven, "Can-Do" attitude, with the ability to multitask.
    • Broad knowledge of the software / game market and platforms.
    • Experience with project management in an Agile development environment (Scrum, Kanban) and experience with using tools that support Agile development (ex. Jira).
    • Ability to create, track, and maintain complex schedules. Strong organizational skills and attention to detail.
    • Passion for gaming and building games.

    • Mobile F2P market knowledge is a plus.
