  • overview

  • We're looking for an experienced and hands-on Principal Software Engineer who wishes to own multiple features and seeks to contribute high quality software on a regular basis and maintain efficient engineering processes. In a small and close-knit team many hats need to be worn and the ideal candidate should feel comfortable with that. The candidate will bring strong technical expertise and will be supported by highly skilled colleagues deeply rooted in the games industry.

  • responsibilities
    • Be responsible for product architecture on the client side and server side both.
    • Be comfortable with monitoring product health based on KPIs and taking appropriate action to address any observed issues.
    • Take ownership of entire features and components of the game. Collaborate with members of the team to deliver large and complex features in time and at very high quality.
    • Be part of the development process and contribute improvements and suggestions, maintain code quality through the whole production cycle.
    • Mentor other developers in the team and help maintain code, product, and process quality.
    • Provide input to Art, Design and Production to ensure schedules and goals meet business and project needs.
    • Conduct regular code reviews of feature code and be part of the hiring process for future colleagues.
    • Identify process improvements where needed and work with the team to drive the implementation of the process improvements.

  • candidate profile
    • Bachelor’s level or equivalent in Computer Science or similar area.
    • 10+ years of relevant experience as a developer.
    • Prior work experience on server side and / or BaaS (Backend as a Service).
    • Experience having lead a team of 3 or more developers.
    • Prior experience having shipped a Free 2 Play game and also having worked on a live game.
    • Advanced knowledge of game development techniques with Unity 3D and C#.
    • Good Knowledge of build / version control, pipeline management and Continuous Integration best practices.
    • Advanced knowledge of the whole game production pipeline and experience working on a live mobile game.
    • Experienced in mentoring other developers in the team.
    • Proactively identify issues with code or processes and collaborate with team to solve them.
    • Excellent written and verbal communication skills in English.
    • Passion for games and the entertainment industry.
    Plusses:
    • Native iOS / Android development experience.
    • Experience with dependency injection frameworks.
    • Knowledge of writing plugins and shaders for Unity.
