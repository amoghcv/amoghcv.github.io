  • overview

  • We are looking for a Data Engineer with passion for video games to join us and to work on data generated by our games. As a Game Data Analyst, you will have the chance to work on some cool titles which have generated large amounts of real world data. Additionally, you get to update the data and the way the game sends data with industry best practices, new ideas, and live operations. In collaboration with both internal and external partners, you will be a core contributor working on game features from beginning to end in a critical data driven role. In a small and close-knit team, at times, many hats may need to be worn and the ideal candidate should feel comfortable with that. The candidate will bring strong technical expertise and will be supported by highly skilled colleagues deeply rooted in the games industry.

  • responsibilities
    • Work with a wide variety of data sources to develop a deep and broad understanding of our games and players.
    • Analyze data quality and ensure that data can be used to provide actionable recommendations to optimize player enjoyment, engagement, and overall efficiencies in all areas of gameplay and player life cycle.
    • Design, develop and maintain data pipelines based on specific business requirements, using data from game servers, web analytics tools, research surveys and financial databases.
    • Provide support for Analytics, Game design, development, testing and operations, including player journey and engagement, gameplay balance, player retention and monetization.
    • Work with Product Managers, Producers and game designers on data requirements and QA.
    • Identify process improvements where needed and work with the team to drive the implementation of the process improvements.
